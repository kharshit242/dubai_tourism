Top 10 Places to Visit in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

The tallest building in the world offering breathtaking views of Dubai.

Burj Al Arab

A luxurious hotel known for its iconic sail-shaped design.

Dubai Mall

One of the largest shopping malls in the world with various attractions.

Dubai Fountain

A mesmerizing water fountain show located near Burj Khalifa.

Palm Jumeirah

An artificial island shaped like a palm tree, home to luxurious resorts.

Dubai Marina

A modern waterfront area with skyscrapers, restaurants, and boat rides.

Ski Dubai

Indoor ski resort inside the Mall of the Emirates.

Dubai Desert Safari

An adventure tour with dune bashing, camel rides, and cultural performances.

Dubai Museum

Learn about Dubai's history and culture at Al Fahidi Fort.

Global Village

A cultural entertainment and shopping destination with pavilions from different countries.
